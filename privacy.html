Data Privacy Policy

Effective February 1, 2025

Our dashboard and machine learning model for predicting/classifying rent trends is developed with robust privacy safeguards. We adhere to Belmont Principles by ensuring respect for persons, beneficence, and justice in all aspects of data handling. Our comprehensive Privacy Impact Assessment leverages Solove's Taxonomy, Nissenbaum's Contextual Integrity, and Mulligan et al.'s Analytic frameworks to rigorously evaluate risks across data collection, storage, processing, and sharing. Detailed risk analysis informs our proactive privacy management strategy, guaranteeing data anonymization, secure storage, and controlled access. Regular audits, transparent policies, and continuous staff training ensure ongoing compliance with evolving industry standards, fostering stakeholder trust and promoting ethical data practices. We remain steadfastly committed to continuous improvement and adherence to best practices in privacy protection.

Our dashboard utilizes public rental property information. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected; all data fields, including addresses, zip codes, and property details, are publicly available. For any inquiries regarding our data practices or privacy safeguards, please contact realpage@critiquant.com or reach out via our individual contact information. Your trust and privacy remain our highest priority.

For more information, please refer to our research paper.

1. Introduction

Welcome to the RealPage Rent Impact Dashboard (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and machine learning dashboard for rent trend prediction and classification.

By accessing or using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use immediately.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We are committed to privacy and transparency in data handling. Our platform primarily utilizes publicly available rental property information and does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) from users.

2.1. Information We Collect

  • Public Property Data: Address, zip code, rental price, property features, historical trends.
  • Non-Personal Usage Data: Analytics on how users interact with our dashboard (e.g., page views, search queries, session duration).
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: We may use cookies or similar tools to improve user experience and collect aggregated analytics.

2.2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, emails, phone numbers, or payment details.
  • Any sensitive information related to race, ethnicity, religion, gender identity, health, or financial status.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used strictly for:

  • Enhancing our rent trend prediction models.
  • Improving dashboard functionality and user experience.
  • Monitoring site performance and security.
  • Conducting research and analysis to refine predictive accuracy.

We do not sell, rent, or trade any data with third parties.

4. Data Protection & Security Measures

We prioritize data security and implement industry-standard safeguards, including:

  • Anonymization & Aggregation: Any collected data is de-identified to prevent traceability to individuals.
  • Encryption: Secure storage and transmission of data using encryption protocols.
  • Access Controls: Restricted access to authorized personnel only.
  • Regular Audits: Periodic assessments to ensure compliance with privacy frameworks.

5. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We do not share or sell user data to third parties. However, we may engage trusted third-party service providers for:

  • Hosting and cloud storage.
  • Site analytics and performance monitoring.

These providers are contractually obligated to follow strict privacy and security guidelines.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance site functionality and gather anonymized insights. You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings.

7. User Rights & Choices

Although we do not collect PII, we respect users' rights concerning data privacy:

  • Access & Transparency: Users can inquire about the data we use.
  • Opt-Out: You may disable cookies or opt out of tracking analytics.
  • Data Deletion Requests: If any information is inadvertently collected, we will honor deletion requests.

For any inquiries, please contact us at realpage@critiquant.com.

8. Compliance with Legal & Regulatory Standards

Our privacy practices align with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - for users in the EU.
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) - for California residents.
  • Other relevant data protection laws as applicable.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date.
